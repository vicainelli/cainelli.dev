When I joined Vivo, the self-service app was still heavily used but hard to evolve safely.

I led a refactor to Vue and Jest, with a big focus on CSS architecture and reusable components. The goal was simple: make the product easier to maintain without slowing feature work.

The results showed up quickly. Customers could solve more issues without calling support, and support volume dropped.

I also implemented CI/CD with automated tests, which replaced a stressful manual release process. QA cycles got shorter and teams could ship with more confidence.

This role reminded me why product quality matters. Small UX improvements in high-traffic flows can save people time every day.
