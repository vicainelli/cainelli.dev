Mandaê was my first startup experience. In 2016, the company had around 2,000 customers, mostly small e-commerce stores dealing with shipping complexity.

I built a shipment management platform in AngularJS. Logistics sounds simple until you are coordinating multiple carriers, incident handling, and delivery estimates in one place.

I invested early in modular components so the codebase would not collapse under feature pressure. That gave the team a base we could iterate on without rewriting the same patterns every sprint.

Working in that environment made the feedback loop very clear: better architecture meant fewer operational headaches for customers and for our own support team.

Mandaê was acquired by Nuvemshop in 2021. I still carry a lot of what I learned there.
