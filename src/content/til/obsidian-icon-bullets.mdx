I just found out Obsidian supports icon bullets, and I cannot believe I missed it.

I use Obsidian for notes, templates, and meeting logs, but I had no idea you could swap regular checkboxes for icons.

Small tweak, big difference. My checklists feel easier to scan and a bit more fun to use.

Here's a quick screenshot of how it looks:

<Image src={obsidianIconBullets} alt="Obsidian icon bullets" />

And here's a cheat sheet of the icons you can use:

```md
## Basics

- [] to-do
- I/1 incomplete
- [x] done
- [-] canceled
- [>] forwarded
- [<] scheduling

## Extras

- [?] question
- [!] important
- [*] star
- ["] quote
- [l] location
- [b] bookmark
- [i] information
- [S] savings
- [I] idea
- [p] pros
- [c] cons
- [f] fire
- [k] key
- [w] win
- [u] up
- [d] down
```

Source: [Minimal Documentation](https://minimal.guide/checklists)
