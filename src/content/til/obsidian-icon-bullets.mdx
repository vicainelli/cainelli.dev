I just discovered that Obsidian has icon bullets, and honestly, I can't believe I missed this!

I've been using Obsidian for all my notes, templates, and meeting logs (both work and personal), but I never knew you could swap regular checkboxes for these neat little icons.

They make lists look cleaner, softer, and so much more fun to use.
Such a small detail, but it makes my note-taking feel way more organized and alive.

And here's a cheat sheet of the icons you can use:

```md
## Basics

- [] to-do
- I/1 incomplete
- [x] done
- [-] canceled
- [>] forwarded
- [<] scheduling

## Extras

- [?] question
- [!] important
- [*] star
- ["] quote
- [l] location
- [b] bookmark
- [i] information
- [S] savings
- [I] idea
- [p] pros
- [c] cons
- [f] fire
- [k] key
- [w] win
- [u] up
- [d] down
```
